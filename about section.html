About

My name is Skjold Gustafsson-Sherson and I am a student at AIS (Aarhus International School)
and I have made this website, as part of a school project. The task for the project was to find a community that I care
about, and design a product that can solve a problem within that community. I am a Vejle Boldklub fan, which is why I
chose to attempt to solve the problem that some Vejle Boldklub fans on the stands don’t know all the songs/chants, or maybe
they don’t know any at all. I have tried to solve this by creating this website, where one can find all the Vejle Boldklub
songs that I could find. The “Songs” section is where one can find the lyrics to the songs.
